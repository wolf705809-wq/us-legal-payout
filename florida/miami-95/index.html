The I-95 corridor in Miami-Dade County, specifically the high-speed Express Lanes, is a primary zone for multi-vehicle collisions. Due to Florida's unique insurance landscape, victims often struggle to access their full statutory entitlement after a crash.

Florida No-Fault Warning: Florida is a No-Fault State. Your $10,000 PIP (Personal Injury Protection) coverage is just the beginning. If your injuries meet the "Permanent Injury Threshold," you can pursue a liability claim for significantly higher damages. Don't let insurers cap your recovery prematurely.

Miami-Specific Recovery Drivers:

  • Trauma Center Records: Claims often involve high-fidelity records from Jackson Memorial Hospital or Mount Sinai Medical Center.
