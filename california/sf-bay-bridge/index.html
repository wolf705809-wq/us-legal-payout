The San Francisco Bay Bridge (I-80) is one of the most high-traffic corridors in Northern California. With its complex upper and lower decks and narrow lane widths, bridge accidents often involve significant liability disputes and complex insurance carrier negotiations.

Bay Area Legal Alert: California's Pure Comparative Negligence rule is vital on the Bay Bridge. Even if road conditions or merging issues contributed to the crash, you have a statutory right to recover damages proportional to the other party's fault.
